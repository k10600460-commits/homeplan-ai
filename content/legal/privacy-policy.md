# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 28, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how SplanAI ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use splanai.com and the related services (the "Service").

## 1. Information We Collect

**Information you provide:**
- **Account information:** name, email address, company name, and password.
- **Billing information:** processed by our payment processor (Stripe). We do not store full payment card numbers on our servers.
- **Content you submit:** property addresses, lot details, and other inputs you enter to generate proposals.
- **Communications:** messages you send us, such as support requests.

**Information collected automatically:**
- **Usage data:** features used, actions taken, and timestamps.
- **Device and log data:** IP address, browser type, operating system, and referring pages.
- **Cookies and similar technologies** (see Section 7).

## 2. How We Use Information

We use information to: provide, operate, and improve the Service; generate proposals; process payments and manage subscriptions; communicate with you and provide support; maintain security and prevent abuse; and comply with legal obligations.

## 3. Service Providers and Third Parties

We share information with service providers who process data on our behalf to operate the Service, including:

- **Stripe** — payment processing
- **Supabase** — database, authentication, and backend hosting
- **Vercel** — application hosting and analytics
- **Google Maps Platform** — location and geocoding
- **RentCast** — property and market data
- **Trestle / CoreLogic** — MLS data (where you connect your own MLS license)
- **Anthropic** — AI generation

**We do not sell your personal information.** We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, our users, or the public.

## 4. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. You may request deletion as described in Section 6.

## 5. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect your information, including authentication controls, access restrictions, encryption of sensitive credentials, and rate limiting. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your information, and to object to or restrict certain processing.

**California residents (CCPA/CPRA):** You have the right to know what personal information we collect, to request deletion, to correct inaccurate information, and to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information. We do not sell your personal information. You will not be discriminated against for exercising these rights.

To exercise any right, contact us at hello@splanai.com.

## 7. Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies for authentication, preferences, and analytics. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling some cookies may affect functionality.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The Service is intended for businesses and users 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or under 16 where applicable).

## 9. International Users

We operate from Japan and serve users in the United States. By using the Service, you understand that your information may be processed in countries that may have data-protection laws different from those in your own country.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version with a new "Last updated" date and, where appropriate, notify you.
